# Robotics in Manufacturing & Industry 4.0

## CodeAlpha Internship Project

### Overview

This repository contains a comprehensive research report on **Robotics in Manufacturing & Industry 4.0**, prepared as part of the **CodeAlpha Internship Program**.

The report explores how robotics has transformed modern manufacturing by improving productivity, efficiency, quality, and workplace safety. It also examines the integration of robotics with advanced technologies such as Artificial Intelligence (AI), Internet of Things (IoT), Cloud Computing, Machine Learning, and Smart Manufacturing systems that form the foundation of Industry 4.0.

As industries continue to adopt automation and intelligent systems, robotics has become a key driver of innovation and industrial growth. This research provides valuable insights into the history, applications, benefits, challenges, case studies, and future trends of robotics in manufacturing environments.

---

## Objectives

The primary objectives of this research are:

* To understand the role of robotics in modern manufacturing.
* To study the evolution of industrial robotics.
* To explore the relationship between robotics and Industry 4.0.
* To analyze various applications of robotic systems in industries.
* To identify the benefits and challenges associated with robotic implementation.
* To examine future developments and emerging trends in industrial automation.
